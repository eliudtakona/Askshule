1. Introduction

AskShule ("we", "our", "us") is a WhatsApp-based admissions and parent engagement solution operated by Infomax Digital Solutions, registered in Kenya. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information when schools and parents use our platform.

By using AskShule, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Parent information: Name, phone number, WhatsApp ID, grade of interest, and any information shared during a WhatsApp conversation with a school's bot.
  • School information: School name, location, fee structure, bus routes, term dates, and admissions details provided by the school administrator.
  • Usage data: Message timestamps, conversation logs, lead status, and interaction data used to improve the service.
  • Payment information: School subscription payments including M-Pesa transaction IDs and bank reference numbers. We do not store card numbers or full bank account details.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the AskShule WhatsApp bot service
  • To generate and deliver admission leads to subscribed schools
  • To notify school administrators of new parent inquiries
  • To process subscription payments and issue invoices
  • To send service updates and term reminders to schools
  • To comply with legal obligations under Kenyan law

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With schools: Parent inquiry data is shared with the school whose bot the parent contacted.
  • With Meta (Facebook): Message data passes through the WhatsApp Business API operated by Meta Platforms Inc. Meta's own privacy policy applies to messages in transit.
  • With service providers: Hosting (Vercel), analytics, and payment processors used to operate AskShule.
  • With legal authorities: If required by Kenyan law or a valid court order.

5. Data Retention

We retain conversation logs and lead data for up to 24 months to provide schools with historical admissions data. Schools may request deletion of their data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

Under Kenyan data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent to data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@askshule.com

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data including encrypted data transmission, secure cloud hosting, and access controls. However no internet transmission is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

AskShule collects information about children only in the context of school admissions, shared by parents or guardians. We do not knowingly collect data directly from children under 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Schools will be notified of significant changes via WhatsApp or email. Continued use of AskShule after changes constitutes acceptance.
